Wholesale4Life

Wholesale4Life is a project I just recently started where I look at and analyze seller's houses, and we discuss a price to sell for. Then we sign a contract for the price you want to sell your house for, and after that I do all the work for you. I will find a buyer for you, and if I am unable to find someone to buy your house in whatever amount of time you give me, you don't have to pay me one penny! This is the best way to sell your house fast and the easiest way to sell your house without the usual long and dreadful process that many sellers have to go through. The Creator

Kaden Rhew

My name is Kaden Rhew and I started Wholesale4Life as a way to help in my efforts to save up for a car. Since I've started, my eyes have really been opened to the real estate world and now my passion is helping people all across NC sell their houses, no matter what condition they're in. Whatever it is, whether it may be property damage, family problems, or just the desire to move to a better place, we will find a price together and I will find the perfect buyer for you. For every seller, there is a buyer, and my sole focus is to find the perfect one for your wants and needs. I am at your service!
